# Privacy Policy for AS IS


**Effective Date:** January 2025  
**Last Updated:** January 2025


## Introduction


AS IS ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").


By using AS IS, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.


---


## 1. Information We Collect


### 1.1 Information You Provide
- **Goal Name**: The name of your goal/cycle when you create a new challenge
- **Notes**: Optional micro-journal notes you write during daily check-ins (max 120 characters)
- **Cycle Duration**: The duration you select for your challenges (3 days, weekend, 1 week, 2 weeks, 1 month, 3 months)


### 1.2 Automatically Collected Information
- **Usage Data**: Information about your daily check-ins, cycle completions, and failure counts
- **Device Information**: Basic device identifiers for RevenueCat purchase verification (anonymous)
- **Purchase History**: Information about your premium purchase through RevenueCat (for entitlement verification only)


### 1.3 Information We Do NOT Collect
- We do NOT collect your name, email address, or phone number
- We do NOT collect your location data
- We do NOT collect photos, videos, or camera access
- We do NOT collect contacts or calendar information
- We do NOT track you across other apps or websites


---


## 2. How We Use Your Information


We use the collected information for the following purposes:


- **App Functionality**: To provide core features like cycle tracking, daily check-ins, and progress monitoring
- **Local Storage**: All your goals, notes, and progress are stored locally on your device using SharedPreferences
- **Purchase Verification**: To verify your premium lifetime access through RevenueCat
- **Anti-Cheat System**: To prevent manipulation of cycle data and ensure fair challenge completion
- **App Improvement**: To understand how users interact with the app and improve features


---


## 3. Data Storage and Security


### 3.1 Local Storage
- All your personal data (goals, notes, check-ins) is stored **locally on your device**
- We do NOT store your data on our servers
- Your data remains on your device and is NOT synced to the cloud


### 3.2 Third-Party Services
We use the following third-party services:


#### **RevenueCat** (In-App Purchases)
- Purpose: To manage premium lifetime access purchases
- Data Collected: Anonymous device identifier, purchase status
- Privacy Policy: [https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy](https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy)


#### **Apple App Store / Google Play Store**
- Purpose: To process payments for premium purchases
- Data Collected: Managed by Apple/Google according to their policies
- Apple Privacy: [https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/)
- Google Privacy: [https://policies.google.com/privacy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)


### 3.3 Security Measures
- We use `flutter_secure_storage` to securely store sensitive data like device fingerprints
- All data is stored locally and protected by your device's security (PIN, biometrics, etc.)
- We do NOT transmit your personal data over the internet


---


## 4. Data Retention


- **Local Data**: Your data remains on your device until you delete the app or clear app data
- **Purchase Data**: RevenueCat retains purchase history for entitlement verification as long as your account exists
- **Deletion**: You can delete all your data at any time by uninstalling the app


---


## 5. Children's Privacy


AS IS does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age. The app is intended for general audiences and does not contain age-restricted content.


---


## 6. Your Rights


You have the following rights regarding your data:


- **Access**: You can view all your data within the app (goals, notes, progress)
- **Delete**: You can delete all your data by uninstalling the app
- **Restore Purchases**: You can restore your premium purchase on a new device using RevenueCat's restore functionality
- **Opt-Out**: You can stop using the app at any time


---


## 7. International Users


AS IS is available worldwide. Your data is stored locally on your device in your country/region. For RevenueCat purchase verification, data may be processed in the United States according to RevenueCat's privacy policy.
